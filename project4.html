Hydrology

Hydrology?

Hydrology is a vital field of concern that deals with one of the most valuable resources on the planet: water. Experts from a variety of fields, ranging from geologists to engineers, study all elements of the Earth's available water in order to get the data needed to monitor this valuable resource. Hydrologists rely on their knowledge of how water interacts with its environment, specifically how it flows from the Planet's surface to the atmosphere and back again.

Economic Opportunies

Hydroelectric energy, also known as hydroelectric power or hydroelectricity, is a type of energy that uses the power of moving water to generate electricity, such as water running over a waterfall. For years, people have harnessed this force. Hydroelectric energy is the most widely used renewable energy source. China is the world's leading generator of hydroelectricity.
